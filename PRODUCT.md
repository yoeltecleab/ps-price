# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PlayStation gamers and deal hunters who want to track PS Store prices without manually checking the store. They use the app at home on desktop or phone, often during sale events or when waiting for a specific game to hit a target price.

## Product Purpose

PS Price monitors PlayStation Store game prices, records history, and sends email alerts when prices drop or reach a target. Success means accurate, up-to-date prices with reliable notifications and zero IP blocking from aggressive scraping.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, electric. A premium gaming command center — not a generic SaaS dashboard. Feels like the PS5 UI evolved: dark, luminous, data-forward.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream-and-purple dashboards
- Cluttered deal-aggregator sites with ad banners
- Over-glassmorphic crypto dashboards
- PlayStation.com marketing pages (too editorial, not a tool)

## Design Principles

1. Data first — prices and trends are the hero, decoration is minimal
2. Trust through precision — real numbers, clear timestamps, honest error states
3. Task flow over spectacle — search, track, alert in under three interactions
4. Dark-native — designed for evening gaming sessions, not daylight office use
5. Motion conveys state — loading, success, price drops; never decorative choreography

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA target. High contrast on dark surfaces, focus-visible rings on all interactive elements, reduced-motion alternatives for all animations, semantic landmarks and form labels throughout.
